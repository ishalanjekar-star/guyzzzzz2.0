Welcome to Digital Footprint — Clarity
A friendly, offline-first tool that helps you understand your online exposure and gives tailored steps to improve your privacy and security.

What is a Digital Footprint?

All the traces you leave while using the internet — social posts, photos, app permissions, search history, and more. These traces can reveal personal patterns and sensitive details.

Why it matters

Employers, scammers, and advertisers can infer things from your footprint. Small changes (like strong passwords and 2FA) give big protection gains.

How this app helps

Quick quiz, personalized feedback, and a local-only history per email. No servers — your data stays on your browser unless you export it yourself.

Who should use it?

Everyone — from teens posting photos to adults using online banking. The app is beginner-friendly and actionable.

History & Context

Websites and apps grew quickly, and we started leaving ever-more detailed traces. Early social networks emphasized sharing; today's challenge is controlling what is shared. This tool aims to make that control approachable.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data uploaded?+
No — everything is stored locally in your browser. Each email's history is stored locally and only visible from the same browser.
How private is the history?+
Private to this browser and the email you use. If someone uses the same browser and email, they will see the same history — so protect your device.
Can I delete my history?+
Yes — go to History while logged in and clear entries individually or all at once.
How does the quiz work?+
The quiz asks about your online habits. Each answer contributes to a score that reflects your digital footprint risk. After submitting, you’ll get personalized tips.
What is a digital footprint?+
Your digital footprint is all the traces you leave online, including posts, photos, app permissions, and browsing habits. It shapes what others can infer about you.
Is this app safe for beginners?+
Absolutely. The app is designed to be beginner-friendly, providing simple explanations and actionable advice without overwhelming technical terms.
